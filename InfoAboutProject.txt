================================================================================
                          CORE FUNCTIONAL ECOSYSTEM
================================================================================

1. AI RESUME COACHING:
   Users upload their resume in PDF format. The `PDFParse` class extracts raw text, 
   and the AI Resume Agent parses it into structured skills, experience, and 
   projects. The Adaptive Mock Interview Agent then customizes questions specifically
   tailored to the user's background.

2. ADAPTIVE LIVE INTERVIEW SANDBOX:
   Simulates full technical and behavioral interviews. Features a splitscreen coding 
   environment with multiple programming language options and a local compiler 
   response feedback interface. It evaluates logic, complexity, and coding style.

3. STRUCTURED REPORTING & SCORING:
   Graded with a readiness score, complete with detailed indicators of strengths, 
   weaknesses, gaps, and exact actionable suggestions.

4. ROADMAPS & ADAPTIVE STUDY PLANS:
   Based on interview results, the Coaching Agent automatically generates study phases 
   with checkpoints, resource recommendations, and checklists to guide users on 
   improving their weak areas.
